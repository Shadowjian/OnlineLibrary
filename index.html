Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix

Author: J.K. Rowling

Review

In what would mark the final director change for the series, David Yates makes his debut with the politically-tinged Order of the Phoenix. The film also happens to be the only Harry Potter movie not written by Steve Kloves (Michael Goldenberg took on scripting duties when Kloves declined to return, though he quickly changed his mind and was back for Half-Blood Prince), but the team forged on to craft the most adult entry yet.
